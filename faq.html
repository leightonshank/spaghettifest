Frequently Asked Questions

What is Spaghettifest?
Spaghettifest is one of the great home-grown music festivals of the Shenandoah Valley. Hosted by the funk mob Midnight Spaghetti & The Chocolate G-Strings, the annual charitable event brings together a thriving community of music lovers and outstanding bands of innumerable genres. This year, the festival proudly moves to Broadway, VA, providing the perfect setting for a weekend of camping, friends, and great performances. Spaghettifest boasts three stages with over 40 bands along with artisans, vendors, and art installations. Each year, all festival proceeds are donated to a selected charity. This year's benefitting charity will be announced in the coming weeks.
When and where is Spaghettifest this year?
Spaghettifest will take place on September 27-29, 2012 at 12480 Mountain Valley Rd, Broadway, VA.
Can I have my pre-order tickets mailed to me at my physical address?
No, all tickets sold online during the pre-sale are WILL CALL ONLY. Your tickets will be held at the door and can be claimed with a photo ID and/or a copy of your purchase receipt.
Can I have my pre-order tickets sent to me electronically?
Once again, no, all tickets sold online during the pre-sale are WILL CALL ONLY.
How can I get a physical ticket?
You will be able to get a physical ticket at will call if you purchase your ticket online; physical tickets are also available through bands and various Midnight Spaghetti-approved brokers.
Is camping included in the ticket price?
Yes. Purchase your pre-sale will call tickets (that include camping) here.
Something came up and I can no longer attend Spaghettifest. Can you refund my ticket?
Sorry for the inconvenience, but ALL SALES ARE FINAL - TICKETS ARE NON-REFUNDABLE.
How do I get my band to play at Spaghetifest?
Bands must submit a hard copy press kit to Midnight Spaghetti Productions, 730 Wyndham Woods Circle, Harrisonburg, VA 22801.
How can my company participate as a vendor at Spaghettifest?
All vendors must obtain a SPAGHETTIFEST VENDING PERMIT. All food vending availabilities have been now been filled. Unauthorized vending may result in removal from the festival.
